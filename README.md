# MediTrack-Video

Do you often forget to take your medicines on time? Missing doses can harm your health. **MediTrack** is a smart medicine reminder app that keeps you on track. Add your medicines and schedule, get timely reminders, track your history, and receive alerts for missed doses. Stay healthy with MediTrack.
